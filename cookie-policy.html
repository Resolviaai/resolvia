This Cookie Policy explains how Resolvia ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve user experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the operation of our website and core functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous data.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember choices you make (e.g., language preferences).
  • Third-Party Cookies: May be set by services we use such as analytics, video embeds, or scheduling tools.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. You may choose to block or delete cookies, but please note this may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Services

We may use tools like Google Analytics or embedded content (e.g., Calendly, YouTube) which place their own cookies. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing their policies.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it regularly to stay informed about how we use cookies.
